Our Calling

A Mission That Changes Lives

TJ BROADCAST was born out of a deep conviction that the youth of this generation need to encounter the living God — not through religion, but through relationship. We exist to bridge the gap between young hearts and God's eternal Word, making truth accessible, relevant, and transformative. Every video, every message, every post carries one aim: to help young people discover who they are in Christ.
